How Does GPT Learn to Talk?

Posted 45 minutes ago

GPT is trained on billions of words from the internet using a technique called “unsupervised learning.” It plays a giant word-guessing game, learning grammar, facts, and style by predicting text. But it has no awareness of truth or meaning—just statistics.
